REFUND POLICY

Our 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

1. Contact our customer support team within 30 days of your purchase
2. Provide your order number and reason for the refund
3. Return the item in its original condition if applicable
4. Once we receive your return, we'll process the refund within 5-7 business days

REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Your refund request is eligible if:
- The request is made within 30 days of purchase
- The item is in original condition with all original packaging
- For digital products, if they have not been downloaded or accessed more than once
- For services, if they have not been fully utilized

ITEMS NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND

- Items purchased more than 30 days ago
- Clearance or final sale items
- Gift cards
- Customized or personalized items that have been completed
- Items that show signs of heavy use or damage

REFUND PROCESS TIMELINE

1. Submit refund request: Immediately eligible
2. Return item (if applicable): 10-15 business days
3. Inspection and approval: 5-7 business days
4. Refund processed: 5-7 business days after approval

Total estimated time: 20-30 business days

PARTIAL REFUNDS

We may offer partial refunds for items that have been used. The amount will be determined based on the condition of the item and how much it has been used.

SHIPPING COSTS

Original shipping costs are non-refundable. However, if the item arrived damaged or defective, we will cover return shipping costs.

QUESTIONS?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact our customer support team at support@company.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
