SHIPPING POLICY

STANDARD SHIPPING INFORMATION

We offer fast and reliable shipping to customers throughout the United States and internationally. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping options.

SHIPPING OPTIONS AND DELIVERY TIMES

1. Standard Shipping (5-7 Business Days)
   - Cost: FREE for orders over $50, $5.99 for orders under $50
   - Delivery: 5-7 business days from order placement
   - Best for: Non-urgent orders

2. Express Shipping (2-3 Business Days)
   - Cost: $12.99
   - Delivery: 2-3 business days from order placement
   - Best for: Urgent orders

3. Overnight Shipping (Next Business Day)
   - Cost: $24.99
   - Delivery: Next business day by 5:00 PM
   - Available for: Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST

4. International Shipping
   - Cost: Varies by location
   - Delivery: 10-21 business days
   - Includes: Customs documentation and tracking

SHIPPING COSTS

Orders within the Continental United States:
- Under $50: Standard shipping $5.99, Express $12.99, Overnight $24.99
- $50-$100: Free standard, Express $12.99, Overnight $24.99
- Over $100: Free standard, Free express, Overnight $24.99

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout and vary based on:
- Destination country
- Package weight
- Selected shipping method

Orders are subject to customs duties and taxes that may apply upon delivery.

TRACKING YOUR ORDER

All orders include free tracking. You will receive:
- Order confirmation email immediately
- Shipping notification with tracking number within 24 hours
- Delivery confirmation upon arrival

Track your order at: www.company.com/track

DELIVERY ADDRESS

- Residential addresses: Delivery at your address
- Business addresses: Delivery Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM
- PO Boxes: Not available for Express or Overnight shipping

HOLIDAY SHIPPING

During peak seasons (November-December and holidays):
- Standard shipping may take 7-10 business days
- Express shipping may take 3-4 business days
- Orders placed after December 15 may not arrive before Christmas

DAMAGED OR LOST PACKAGES

If your package arrives damaged or is lost:
1. Contact us immediately with photos and tracking number
2. We will file a claim with the carrier
3. You will receive a replacement or full refund
4. Process typically takes 5-10 business days

SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS

We cannot ship to:
- P.O. Boxes (except standard ground shipping)
- Military addresses (except APO/FPO with Express or Overnight)
- Certain restricted items to specific states

RETURNS SHIPPING

Return shipping policies:
- We provide a prepaid return label for defective items
- For refunds, customers pay return shipping unless product is defective
- International returns must be arranged with customer service
