PASSWORD RESET GUIDE

FORGOT YOUR PASSWORD?

Don't worry! Resetting your password is quick and easy. Follow these steps to regain access to your account.

STEPS TO RESET YOUR PASSWORD

Method 1: Using Email (Most Common)

1. Go to the login page at www.company.com/login
2. Click on "Forgot your password?" link
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Click "Send Reset Email"
5. Check your email (including spam folder) for a password reset link
6. Click the link in the email (link expires in 24 hours)
7. Enter your new password (must be at least 8 characters)
8. Confirm your new password
9. Click "Reset Password"
10. You will be redirected to login with your new password

Method 2: Using Your Username

1. Visit www.company.com/login
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Select "Use username instead"
4. Enter your username
5. Click "Send Reset Email"
6. Follow steps 5-10 from Method 1

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS

Your new password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Contain at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- Contain at least one number (0-9)
- Contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Not match any of your previous 5 passwords
- Not contain your username

PASSWORD RESET EMAIL

After clicking "Send Reset Email," you should receive an email from: noreply@company.com

Subject: Password Reset Request - Action Required

If you don't see the email:
1. Check your spam or junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes for delivery
3. Try resetting again
4. Check if you used a different email address for registration

RESET LINK EXPIRATION

Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link has expired:
1. Go back to the login page
2. Click "Forgot your password?"
3. Request a new reset email

SECURITY TIPS

When resetting your password:
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your password with anyone
- Don't use personal information (name, birthday, etc.)
- Avoid using common words or patterns
- Don't use the same password across multiple websites
- Change your password regularly

ACCOUNT SECURITY

Protect your account:
- Use two-factor authentication (2FA) if available
- Verify email and phone number in account settings
- Check login activity in your account
- Log out from other devices if needed
- Update password every 90 days

STILL CAN'T RESET?

If you're having issues with password reset:

1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different web browser
3. Try from a different device
4. Disable browser extensions/ad blockers
5. Make sure you're using the correct email

If problems persist, contact support:
- Email: support@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-HELP-NOW
- Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

ACCOUNT LOCKED

Your account may be temporarily locked if multiple failed login attempts are detected. In this case:
1. Wait 30 minutes before trying again
2. Use the password reset feature
3. Contact support if the issue continues

ACCESSING YOUR ACCOUNT AFTER RESET

1. Go to www.company.com/login
2. Enter your email or username
3. Enter your new password
4. Click "Login"
5. You will be logged into your account

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION

If you have 2FA enabled, after entering your password:
1. You'll be prompted to enter a verification code
2. Check your email or authenticator app for the code
3. Enter the code and click "Verify"
4. You will be logged in

RECOVER YOUR ACCOUNT

If someone else has access to your account:
1. Immediately click "Forgot Password" and reset it
2. Change your security questions
3. Update your recovery email
4. Enable two-factor authentication
5. Contact support immediately at security@company.com

QUESTIONS?

For help with password reset, contact:
- Chat support: www.company.com/chat
- Email: support@company.com
