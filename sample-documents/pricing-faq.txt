PRICING FAQ - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL PRICING QUESTIONS

Q: How much does your service cost?
A: We offer flexible pricing plans starting at $9.99/month for our Basic plan, $29.99/month for Professional, and custom pricing for Enterprise. See our pricing page at www.company.com/pricing for full details.

Q: Do you offer free trials?
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start your trial.

Q: Is there a discount for annual subscriptions?
A: Absolutely! Annual subscriptions get a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. You save $24 per year on Basic, $72 per year on Professional, and more on higher tiers.

Q: Can I change my plan anytime?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any credits or charges.

Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?
A: Yes! We offer 50% discounts for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact sales@company.com with proof of status.

BILLING AND PAYMENTS

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Q: When will I be charged?
A: Monthly plans are charged on the same day each month. Annual plans are charged once per year. Charges appear in your billing statement.

Q: Do I need to provide a credit card for the free trial?
A: No, our free trial requires no credit card. You can add a payment method when you're ready to upgrade.

Q: What happens to my account if payment fails?
A: We'll send you email reminders. Your account will be suspended after 5 days of failed payment. Contact billing@company.com to update your payment method.

Q: Is there a setup fee?
A: No, there are no setup fees or hidden charges. You only pay for your monthly or annual subscription.

Q: Do you offer invoicing?
A: Yes! Enterprise customers can request invoicing. Contact billing@company.com for details.

REFUNDS AND CANCELLATIONS

Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Q: Will I get a refund if I cancel?
A: Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable. For annual plans, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact support@company.com to request a refund.

Q: What happens to my data when I cancel?
A: You'll have 30 days to download your data after cancellation. After 30 days, we'll delete your account and data permanently.

Q: Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?
A: Yes! You can pause your subscription for up to 3 months. During this time, you won't be charged, but your account will be inactive.

UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE

Q: How do I upgrade my plan?
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Current Plan and click "Upgrade." Choose your new plan, and you'll receive credits for the remainder of your current billing period.

Q: How are upgrades prorated?
A: If you upgrade mid-month, we'll charge the difference between plans for the remaining days. The credit appears in your next billing cycle.

Q: Can I downgrade my plan?
A: Yes, downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. You won't lose any data.

Q: What if I downgrade and exceed the feature limits?
A: You'll have 30 days to delete content that exceeds your new plan's limits. Contact support@company.com if you need help.
